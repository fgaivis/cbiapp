How to Donate

At CBI we believe who's at the table makes a difference. When we come to the table with a diverse group of people, voices and experiences, the differences that define us as individuals create synergy. Our community is stronger when our shared commitment and capacity for inclusion and equity is increased. This long-term work needs your support. Please "pull up a chair" and join us by making your gift today.
